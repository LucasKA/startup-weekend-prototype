Public Star Party

Griffith Observatory

Friends Joining you:

  • Elizabeth Pezza

Saturday, November 21 | 2-9:45 PM, FREE

One Saturday a month, the Observatory hosts a public star party. Free public star parties are held monthly at Griffith Observatory with the assistance of volunteers from the Los Angeles Astronomical Society, the Los Angeles Sidewalk Astronomers,and The Planetary Society.

They are a chance for the whole family to look at the Sun, Moon, visible planets and other objects, to try out a variety of telescopes, and to talk to knowledgeable amateur astronomers about the sky and their equipment.

Please be aware that the astronomers and telescope demonstrators must cut off the line for each telescope to enable all viewing to be completed by 9:45 p.m. Hours for telescope operation are not the same as for the building (which closes at 10:00p.m.).
