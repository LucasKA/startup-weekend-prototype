Transforming the Samuel Oschin Planetarium

Griffith Observatory

Saturday, November 21 | 2-9:45 PM, FREE

Friends Of The Observatory and Griffith Observatory have again taken the next step and brought the Samuel Oschin Planetarium to the cutting edge of planetarium education and entertainment.

Come witness this major development in Griffith Observatory's history and join Director Dr. E.C. Krupp and Curator Dr. Laura Danly for a demonstration of the newly installed, state-of-the-art equipment in the Samuel Oschin Planetarium. As part of this special audience, you will also get a sneak peek of the 2017 planetarium show in development!

On Monday, November 16, at 7:30 p.m., Observatory staff and fellow FOTO members will gather to see the results of a major technological upgrade to the all-dome digital animation projectors, the visuals, and the dome lighting. This transformation, which creates a more immersive storytelling experience for all our planetarium shows, creates images that "...dazzle with unprecedented clarity, brightness, depth of color, and detail," according to Dr. Krupp. Attendees will get to see the impact of the improvements on Centered in the Universe, Griffith Observatory's signature planetarium show.
